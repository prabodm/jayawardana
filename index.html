A Taste of Authentic Sri Lankan Hospitality

Located on the bustling Ambepussa-Kurunegala Road in Alawwa, Jayawardhana Sweet House & Hotel has been serving the community and travelers alike with delicious Sri Lankan cuisine and freshly made sweets.

Just steps away from Alawwa Railway Station, we're the perfect stop for a hearty breakfast, a quick meal between journeys, or a leisurely dining experience with family and friends.
